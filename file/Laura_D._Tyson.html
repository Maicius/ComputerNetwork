Faculty and Executive Leadership Directory

Laura D. Tyson

Interim Dean
Distinguished Professor of the Graduate School
Faculty Director, Institute for Business & Social Impact
Haas Business and Public Policy Group
510-642-1230
Email: click on the envelope icon below for full email address
Academic Status: On duty
Office Hours: None
Twitter: @LauraDTyson
Curriculum Vitae (in PDF format, Acrobat Reader required)

Education


PhD, Economics, Massachusetts Institute of Technology
BA, Economics, Smith College



Positions Held


At Haas since 1990

2016 – present, Distinguished Professor of the Graduate School

2006 – present, Chair, Board of Trustees, Blum Center for Developing Economies

2013 – present, Faculty Director, Institute for Business & Social Impact

2013 – 2016, Professor of Business Administration and Economics

2008 – 2013, S. K. and Angela Chan Chair in Global Management

2007 – 2008, Professor, Haas School of Business

2002 – 2006, Dean, London Business School
1998 – 2001, Dean, Haas School of Business
1997 – 2001, Principal, Law and Economics Consulting Group
1990 – 2001, Professor, Haas School of Business
1977 – 2001, Professor, Department of Economics, UC Berkeley
1995 – 1996, Chairman, National Economic Council
1993 – 1995, Chairman, President’s Council of Economic Advisers
1990 – 1992, Director of the Institute of International Studies, UC Berkeley
1988 – 1992, Director of Research, Berkeley Roundtable on the International Economy, UC Berkeley
1974 – 1977, Assistant Professor, Department of Economics, Princeton University



External Service and Assignments


  • Former Member, Semiconductor Working Group, President’s Council of Advisors on Science and Technology
  • Former Member, President Obama’s Council on Jobs and Competitiveness
  • Former Member, President’s Economic Recovery Advisory Board
  • Former Member, U.S. Department of State, Foreign Affairs Policy Board
  • Former Board Member, Science, Technology and Economic Policy, The National Academies
  • Board of Trustees, Bay Area Council Economic Institute
  • Organizer, Berkeley Work and Intelligent Tools and Systems (WITS)
  • Member of Corporate Boards: AT&T Inc.; CBRE Group Inc.; Lexmark International Inc.; Apex Swiss Holdings SARL Advisory Board: Generation Investment Management; H&Q Asia Pacific; Observatory Group; Afiniti; Morgan Stanley Institute for Sustainable Investing
  • Advisory Council: The Brookings Institution, Hamilton Project; Social Finance US
  • Chair of Board of Trustees & Steering Committee Member, Blum Center for Developing Economies
  • Senior Advisor: McKinsey Global Institute; The Rock Creek Group;
  • Special Advisor: Mastercard Center for Inclusive Growth; Berkeley Research Group
  • Senior External Advisor, McKinsey Global Institute
  • Economic Advisory Council and Board of Trustees, Center for American Progress
  • Editorial Advisory Board, The International Economy
  • Task Force Member, Rework America, Markle Foundation
  • Co-Chair, Global Future Council on Education, Gender and Work, World Economic Forum
  • Berggruen Institute: Think Long Committee for California; 21st Century Council
  • Economic Advisor, Alliance for Competitive Taxation
  • International Advisory Council, Bocconi University
  • Board member: Opportunity Institute; The Sustainability Accounting Standards Board Foundation


Current Research and Interests


  • Changes in the global economy, with special focus on high-technology competition and disruption from automation and artificial intelligence
  • U.S. trade policy
  • Women’s economic empowerment

  • The New York Times Economix Blog, articles appeared monthly, March 2011 – April 2014.

  • Tyson, Laura and Jennifer Walske. Berkeley Haas Case Studies: Better Ventures: Backing Entrepreneurs, Building a Better World (2017): Bridges Fund Management (2017); Sanergy: Tackling Sanitation in Kenyan Slums (2016); Revolution Foods (2015); Fair Trade USA (2015).
  • Klugman, Jeni and Laura Tyson. Leave No One Behind: A call for action for gender equality and women’s economic empowerment. UN Secretary-General’s High Level Panel on Women’s Economic Empowerment, September 2016.
